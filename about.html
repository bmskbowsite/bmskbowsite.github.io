About Us

KBOW is a show produced and viewed by Bowditch students. The main people who produce it is the 7th Grade Broadcast Journalism Elective, currently taught by Mr. Umberger, but we allow submissions for segments from people who aren't in the class

The 2022 Broadcast Journalism Class

FAQ

What type of things will I see on KBOW?

On KBOW we air whatever things interest our students. We have segments about art, singing, programming, and just general interesting things.

Can I join the KBOW staff?

You can only join the staff if you are signed up for Broadcast Journalism. However, you can submit videos whenever you want!

How frequently are videos released?

Videos are released every Friday, except on weeks with less than 5 days in the week.
